Paper Details

Title: SokoBench: Evaluating Long-Horizon Planning and Reasoning in Large Language Models

Key Findings & Importance

  • Novel Benchmark: Introduces SokoBench, a specialized benchmark using simplified Sokoban puzzles to isolate long-horizon planning from state persistence challenges.
  • Planning Limitations: Identifies a critical constraint where LLM performance degrades significantly when planning requires more than 25 moves, suggesting fundamental limitations in forward planning capacity.
  • Tooling Limited Impact: Shows that even with PDDL tools, improvements are modest, suggesting architectural limitations that scaling alone cannot overcome.
  • Methodological Rigor: The controlled isolation of planning variables provides clear evidence about LLM planning capabilities.
